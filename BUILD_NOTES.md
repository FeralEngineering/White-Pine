# Build Notes

## Prototype Notes

- OLED display cutout was accidentally oversized and corrected with a cardstock bezel.
- Charging status LEDs are mostly hidden inside the enclosure, though light is visible around the USB-C port.
- Internal space was extremely tight and required careful wire routing.
- Lifetime count intentionally includes observations recorded during testing and development.
- Screen remains off during normal operation and only activates on startup or via long button press.
- Built primarily from parts already on hand, including a salvaged vape battery.

## Lessons Learned

- Finalize enclosure openings before wiring components.
- Verify button wiring before closing the enclosure.
- Leave more room for wiring than seems necessary.
- Consider a more integrated charging/power solution in future revisions.**
