# Hardware

The White Pine v1 prototype was built from a mixture of new and salvaged components.

## Electronics

- ESP8266 D1 Mini
- 0.96" SSD1306 OLED display
- TP4056 USB-C charging module
- MT3608 boost converter
- Momentary pushbutton
- Rocker power switch
- LED indicator

## Power

- Salvaged lithium polymer battery recovered from a disposable vape

## Enclosure

- Small plastic project enclosure
